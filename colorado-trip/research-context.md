# BLACK CANYON OF THE GUNNISON NP

- ~1.5hrs from Crested Butte, ~94mi
- **Dog-friendly!** Dogs allowed (leashed) on all roads, campgrounds, overlooks, Cedar Point Nature Trail, North Rim Chasm View Trail, Rim Rock Trail (Aug 1 - May 31).
- 12 scenic overlooks on South Rim Drive. Painted Wall = Colorado's tallest cliff (2,250ft).
- East Portal Road — drive to the Gunnison River at canyon bottom.
- Half day to full day visit.
- No timed entry or reservation needed.

## Desert Driving Safety (Jul-Aug)
- Never leave Mochi in van without A/C running (interior spikes 20°+ in 5min)
- Drive desert segments early morning (depart 6-7am)
- Carry extra water for Mochi (1oz per lb body weight per day, more in heat)
- Test pavement with hand before walking Mochi
- Nevada drives (US-50, US-6) are the hottest
- Camp at elevation when possible
